This app is a workout tracker for strength training. No login required -- the app works entirely offline and requires no account setup.

Core Features:
- Log workouts with sets, weight, reps, and RIR (Reps in Reserve)
- Create custom training programs or choose from 8 built-in templates
- View workout history and track progression over time

To test: Launch the app and tap the "+" button on the home screen to start a new workout. Select exercises from the library, log your sets, and complete the workout. Navigate to "Programs" in the bottom tab bar to browse program templates.

In-App Purchases:
This app offers two types of in-app purchases:

1. Premium Subscription (monthly or annual): Unlocks extended analytics including volume trends, full training history, progression curves, and data-driven deload suggestions. Subscription auto-renews unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

2. Template Pack (one-time purchase): Unlocks 5 additional premium program templates (Periodized Strength 531, Linear Progression LP, Tiered Volume Method, Periodized Hypertrophy, Strength-Endurance Block).

To test premium features: Navigate to "Settings" in the bottom tab bar and tap "Restore Purchases" to verify purchase restoration. Premium features are gated behind the subscription or template pack purchase respectively.

The 3 free program templates (Full Body, Upper / Lower, Push / Pull / Legs) and basic workout tracking are fully functional without any purchase.
